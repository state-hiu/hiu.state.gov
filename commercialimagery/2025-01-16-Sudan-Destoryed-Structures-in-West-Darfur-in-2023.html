Sudan: Destroyed Structures in West Darfur in 2023

The RSF and allied militias began their assault on El Geneina on April 24 and attained full control of the city on June 15, killing the governor. The image on the right shows the changes at El Geneina IDP Camp where approximately 1,350 structures were burned between mid-June 2023 and late August 2023.
